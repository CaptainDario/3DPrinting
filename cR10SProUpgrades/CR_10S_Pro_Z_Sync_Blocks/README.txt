# Summary

Print 2 of these blocks to level the X-axis gantry in the Z direction.
The blocks are large enough to stand on their own, they are keyed to slip into the 2040 extruded, and have notches cutout for the brackets and GT2 belt.

If you were using the included 100mm acrylic gauge before this is much easier.

This may work for other CR-10 models, I am not sure, I only have the Pro.
