==========================


For those that want to upgrade the fans in their Creality CR-10s Pro to the ultra quiet fans, I submit a conversion fan mount.

This mount will allow you to replace the stock 40mm radial fan with a 50x50x10mm Ultra quiet fan.

Inspired by watching this video by Nerys: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RRbvASlom4A
No longer need to secure the fan with sticky tape.

Fan secures to the mount with: 
  (2) 4x12mm bolts. (50x50 ver)
  (2) 3x12mm bolts. (40x40 ver)

Attach the mount to the factory mounting posts with (1)3x12mm bolt.
The hole in back will fit over the post to secure the mount, but no bolt is needed here for support.
